About

As an IT System Administrator with over 5 years of professional experience, my career began with a deep interest in technology and its potential to simplify and enhance lives. Initially, I focused on learning programming and the fundamental principles of systems operations, which helped me build a strong foundation in this field. During my education, I prioritized both theoretical knowledge and practical experience.

Throughout my career, I have worked in various organizations, gaining extensive expertise in setting up network infrastructures, installing servers, and managing virtualization environments. From Windows Server, Linux, and FreeBSD operating systems to network solutions like Cisco and Ubiquiti UniFi, I have successfully managed and optimized complex IT infrastructures.

Automating IT processes is a particular area of interest for me. Using scripting languages like PowerShell, Bash, and Python, I develop solutions that automate routine tasks, boost productivity, and reduce workloads. Additionally, I have engaged in small programming projects, enhancing my skills in C# and .NET Core.

Currently, I focus on IT infrastructure security, network performance optimization, and delivering reliablel IT services to users. Solving challenges in my work is not just a responsibility but also an opportunity to grow and find creative solutions.
